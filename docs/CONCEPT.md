## 1. Executive Conceptual Abstract

*Sino Cicatrizado (The Scarred Bell)* is a site-specific, distributed, hybrid acoustic installation designed for Ouro Preto, Brazil [Ouro Preto...]. Rather than treating the city’s colonial architecture and mountainous topography as a passive backdrop, the apparatus operates as a **System-Environment Hybrid (SEH)** [Emergence and Embodiment]. It entangles the physical movements of participants (somatic nodes), the legacy of Afro-diasporic acoustic resistance (*Linguagem dos Sinos*), and the material limits of contemporary cellular and hardware infrastructure into a single, self-organizing acoustic feedback system [Ouro Preto..., tracing-the-scar].

The system rejects the **erasure paradigm**—the digital fiction of "undo" or "reset" [tracing-the-scar]. Every action performed within the apparatus leaves an irreversible, physical-discursive trace: a **scar** [tracing-the-scar, S33]. The work functions not as a window to look *through* (representing some external history), but as a scarred recording surface to look *at*—evidence of a collaborative, thermodynamic encounter that has permanently altered the system's phase space [tracing-the-scar, S36].

## 2. The Critique of the Clean Loop: From Homeostasis to the Scar

```
THE FOC CIRCLE (Homeostasis / Myth of Reset)
     ,-----,
    '       '  <-- Illusion of perfect return / "Undo"
     `-----'

THE SOC SPIRAL (Hysteresis / Hysteretic Drift)
     ,-----,
    '   ,---'  <-- Every return inherits the sedimented scars of past cycles
     `---'
```

First-order cybernetics (FoC) and modern interactive art are dominated by the **homeostasis trap** [tracing-the-scar]. They assume a feedback loop returns to an ideal, pristine equilibrium [tracing-the-scar]. Noise is treated as pathology, and material substrates are reduced to transparent carriers of pattern [tracing-the-scar, S11]. This hides the physical reality of communication: **information is a physical inscription** (forensic materiality) [tracing-the-scar, S12]. Writing a bit deforms a surface, dissipates heat, and consumes non-recoverable free energy [tracing-the-scar].

*Sino Cicatrizado* shifts the system geometry from the **circle** (reversible return) to the **spiral** (hysteretic progression) [tracing-the-scar]. Built on Heinz von Foerster's formulation of the *non-trivial machine* (NTM), the system's internal state ($z$) is a recursive archive of its own operational history:
$$z_{t+1} = g(x_t, z_t)$$

The system cannot return to its starting state because its physical and database registers have been permanently altered [tracing-the-scar]. **Personality is the accumulation of scars** [tracing-the-scar]. The virtual bells do not play clean, pre-recorded audio; they sound their historical trauma [tracing-the-scar]. When a participant approaches a virtual bell tower, they listen to the microscopic frequency shifts, temporal drifts, and structural changes left behind by every preceding participant [tracing-the-scar].

## 3. System-Environment Hybrids (SEH) & Non-Trivial Agency

Following Mark B. N. Hansen’s critique of autopoietic closure in *Emergence and Embodiment*, we decouple structural closure from absolute isolation [Emergence and Embodiment]. Traditional autopoietic theory positions the environment purely as a source of "perturbations" or "excuses" for internal neural cycles [Emergence and Embodiment, S21]. 

*Sino Cicatrizado* recognizes the **non-trivial agency of the environment** [Emergence and Embodiment]. The physical constraints of Ouro Preto’s topography, network infrastructure, and client hardware are co-composers of the work [Ouro Preto..., tracing-the-scar]:

*   **Topographical Impedance:** Ouro Preto’s extreme verticality shifts the audio filter slopes of the virtual bells [Ouro Preto...]. Gravity and physical exhaustion are mapped directly to Web Audio nodes [tracing-the-scar]. The environment resists; it in-forms the system [Emergence and Embodiment].
*   **The Materiality of the Signal:** We reject the optimization of signal over noise.
    *   *GPS Jitter:* Rather than smoothing over GPS coordinate drift, the app uses this jitter to fluctuate the Euclidean rhythm generators, creating micro-timing syncopations [16, tracing-the-scar].
    *   *Cellular Latency:* Packet dropouts are not compensated for; they are routed into a bit-crusher, producing a raw, digital grit [tracing-the-scar, 3].
*   **Thermodynamic Exhaustion:** The device’s battery level ($E_{\text{battery}}$) acts as a physical system constraint [tracing-the-scar]. As energy dissipates, the sound quality degrades into 4-bit digital dirt [tracing-the-scar]. The system performs the material exhaustion of its own somatic nodes [tracing-the-scar].

## 4. Decolonial Cybernetics & Acoustic Confluence

The system is grounded in the decolonial framework of the late Piauí intellectual **Nêgo Bispo** and the historical Afro-diasporic resistance of Ouro Preto [Ouro Preto...]:

```
  TRANSFLUENCE (Colonial / Assimilation)
  ───────────────────┐
                     ├───────────────────> (Homogenization / Erasure)
  ───────────────────┘

  CONFLUENCE (Decolonial / Hybrid Matters)
  ───────────────────┐
                     ├───────────────────> (Mutual Amplification with Difference)
  ───────────────────┘
```

### 4.1 Confluence over Transfluence
Bispo contrasted *transfluence* (the colonial process of assimilation, extraction, and erasure of difference) with *confluence* (the meeting of differing entities that mutually amplify one another without losing their distinct, intrinsic identities) [Ouro Preto...]. 

*Sino Cicatrizado* acts as an acoustic confluence [Ouro Preto...]. When somatic nodes (participants) cross paths physically, the web app triggers a direct peer-to-peer connection (WebRTC), mixing and modulating their custom reflector parameters without flattening their distinct spectral profiles [Ouro Preto...]. The rivers meet, creating a more complex, unified current, but the sediment of each individual's path remains distinct [Ouro Preto...].

### 4.2 Acoustic Resistance & Chico Rei
The *Toque dos Sinos* in Ouro Preto historically functioned as an informational matrix [Ouro Preto...]. Enslaved *sineiros* (bell ringers), forced to execute rigid European ecclesiastical scores, used the towers to perform acoustic resistance—syncopating the rhythms and injecting Afro-Brazilian polyrhythms to communicate hidden messages of safety, death, and rebellion [Ouro Preto...]. 

The virtual bells of *Sino Cicatrizado* emulate this systemic subversion. The underlying rhythms are Euclidean, based on Toussaint's formalization of Bjorklund's spacing algorithms [16, 48]. However, as participants approach the towers, their local coordinates "irritate" these structures [Ouro Preto..., 11]. The presence of the somatic node injects syncopation and detunes the frequency partials, reclaiming the rigid mathematical algorithm as an site of lived, organic improvisation [Ouro Preto..., tracing-the-scar].

## 5. The Echolocating Soma: Active Observation

In second-order cybernetics, **the observer is placed inside the loop** [tracing-the-scar, S11]. There is no position of immune, detached objectivity [tracing-the-scar]. In *Sino Cicatrizado*, the participant is not a detached user looking at a map on a screen; they are a somatic node whose physical presence co-constitutes the acoustic phenomenon [Ouro Preto..., tracing-the-scar].

```
                     ┌─────────────────────────────┐
                     │    Somatic Chirp (Prober)   │
                     └──────────────┬──────────────┘
                                    │
                                    ▼ (Active Sonar)
                     ┌─────────────────────────────┐
                     │    Propagation delay (d/343)│
                     └──────────────┬──────────────┘
                                    │
                                    ▼
                     ┌─────────────────────────────┐
                     │  Reflected Spectral Echoes  │
                     │  (Warped Historical Trace)  │
                     └─────────────────────────────┘
```

The introduction of **Active Echolocation** re-frames the relationship between the body and space [tracing-the-scar]. When a participant triggers a "chirp" from their mobile device, they emit an acoustic probe into the coordinate field [tracing-the-scar]. This wave travels outward at the physical speed of sound ($343\text{ m/s}$), triggering delayed, refracted echoes from any active towers or participant-placed static reflectors within a $500\text{-meter}$ radius [tracing-the-scar].

This is the **witness-function** made acoustic [tracing-the-scar]. The participant "read-listens" to the unseen historical and somatic density of their surroundings [tracing-the-scar]. They are not navigating a sterile digital twin; they are navigating a scarred landscape whose ghosts reply to their physical touch [Ouro Preto..., tracing-the-scar].

## 6. The Sovereign Void & Self-Limitation

Our control architecture integrates a vital limit case: **the system must possess the right to decline optimization** [tracing-the-scar].

Drawing on crip-theory (the critique of the "ideology of cure") and Dine (Navajo) cosmology's *Hozho* (restoring balance without leaving a permanent monument of trauma), the apparatus incorporates **self-limitation** [tracing-the-scar]. If the collective participant density at a single coordinate becomes too high, the local acoustic impedance scales to infinity [Ouro Preto..., tracing-the-scar]. Rather than attempting to "heal" this congestion through load-balancing or volume normalization, **the system scars itself** [tracing-the-scar]. It permanently deadens that node, rendering it silent [tracing-the-scar].

This **sovereign void** serves as a structural warning [tracing-the-scar]. It reminds the participant that every agential cut has an asymmetric cost—that the resources of our technologies are not infinite, and that some wounds cannot be cured, only tended [tracing-the-scar, S33].

## 7. The Evolving and the Archived: Dual-Twin Ontology

Finally, the system addresses the problem of memory through a bifurcated database architecture [Emergence and Embodiment, tracing-the-scar]:

*   **The Living City (Autopoiesis):** This is the live, continuously mutating state of the city’s virtual bells [Ouro Preto..., tracing-the-scar]. It is a process of ongoing structural drift, reflecting the continuous, chaotic passages of the hybrid collective [tracing-the-scar, 15].
*   **The Scarred Twin (The Archived Monument):** This is an immutable, read-only snapshot taken at the final moment of the ASC 2026 conference [Ouro Preto..., tracing-the-scar]. It frozen-records the exact state of the system’s scars [tracing-the-scar]. It does not evolve [tracing-the-scar].

By choosing between these two databases, the participant can interact with either the *Living City* (ongoing, irreversible becoming) or the *Scarred Twin* (the frozen historical monument) [Ouro Preto..., tracing-the-scar]. The work thus preserves its own history as an active, queryable archaeological trace, demonstrating how neocybernetics can rescue agency and memory in a world dominated by the illusion of the clean slate [Emergence and Embodiment, tracing-the-scar].
